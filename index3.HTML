About Dance

Dance has been a central part of my training for many years. I have participated in multiple competitions, performing in both troupe and solo categories, as well as taking part in various auditions, which have strengthened my stage presence and adaptability.

I am primarily a contemporary dancer, with a strong technical foundation and training in ballet, heels, and jazz. This diversity of styles allows me to approach movement with versatility, precision, and performance awareness.

Through continuous training and performance experiences, I aim to further develop my technique and artistic range while pursuing opportunities in professional dance environments.
